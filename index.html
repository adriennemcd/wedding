Parking/Shuttle

There are three options for parking in and around Awbury: Station Rd (15 spots), the SEPTA Washington Lane train station parking lot, and street parking (we recommend Washington Lane or Chew Avenue). Please click on the image below to see a map of Awbury with parking options.

Parking
To see if any of the Station Rd. spots are available, please enter Awbury at the main entrance on Chew Ave. and bear left at the fork. You'll reach the parking attendant at the Station Rd. junction, who will either direct you to a spot on Station Rd., or down to the train station parking lot. If both of those options are full, there should be plenty of street parking along Washington Lane and Chew Avenue.

Walking or Shuttle
Once you park, you have the option of walking up to Cope house or taking a shuttle. If you plan to walk, you can enter Awbury at the Station Rd. entrance (located in the Washington Lane train station parking lot) or at Awbury's main entrance on Chew Ave., whichever is closest. Just be aware on Station Rd. that there may be cars driving in the opposite direction than you.

If you plan to take the shuttle, walk to the pick up point in the Washington Lane train station parking lot (there will be a sign). The van will be doing a continuous loop, so if it's not there, just hang tight for a few minutes.

Either way, let's all cross our fingers for good weather. If that doesn't work and it's rainy, don't forget to bring your umbrellas and dress comfortably!
